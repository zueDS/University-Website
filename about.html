Go Beyond the Books

Internships & Co-ops

Try out a career and get the professional experience you need — before you graduate. WSU's internships and co-ops connect students with employers in their areas of interest, where they can build a resume, earn academic credit and a paycheck.

Study Abroad

Thanks to WSU's extensive study-abroad connections, you can take your WSU education on the road and swap campuses, countries and culture — for a summer, semester or full academic year — while earning credit toward your degree and paying WSU tuition.

Student Outcomes

Where can a Wichita State education take you? Just ask our current students and recent grads who’ve taken advantage of our unmatched opportunities to discover their dream careers, conduct groundbreaking research and, ultimately, make a positive impact on their futures—and the world.

Invested in your success

The transition to college life can be challenging. WSU's Office of Student Success is proactive in helping all students maximize their potential and reach their personal goals. Student Success offers personal success coaches, tutoring services and financial advisors to support students straight through from orientation to graduation.
